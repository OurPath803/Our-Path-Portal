The Gift Series · Design System

TERRA
Brand Foundations

The visual language for OurPath's Gift Series workshop pack, a quiet, scholarly system that holds classical psychospiritual tradition and Western developmental science in one calm frame. Earth, ink, and gold. Built to feel premium and unhurried, never corporate or hyped.

01

Logo & wordmark

The lockup pairs the OurPath compass mark, a journey path read through a crosshair, with “OurPath” in Cormorant Garamond and the tagline Guidance & Mentoring in small-caps. Use the teal version on cream, the light version on teal. Clear space ≥ the mark height on all sides.

Do

Place top-centre or top-left on every cover, poster, slide master, and document header. Keep the mark, wordmark, and tagline locked together as one unit.

Don't

Don't recolour the wordmark, stretch it, set it on busy imagery, or drop the mark below ~24px where the journey path stops reading.

02

Palette

TERRA base carries authority and calm. Each gift then earns its own accent, layered over the base so the four read as a family yet stay individually recognisable.

04

The four gifts

One motif runs through everything: Mind · Heart · Soul · Hands. Each gift maps to a domain, an embedded OurPath tool, and a week of the journey. The icons are fine line marks, recoloured to each gift's accent.

The arc: Mind (see clearly) → Heart (name what you carry & reconnect) → Soul (integrate meaning) → Hands (choose and do). This is also the order of OurPath's four tools, experienced in sequence.

05

Slide master & document chrome

On-screen pieces run on a navy master with large legible type for Google Meet. Print documents use a cream canvas with a gold-ruled header and the non-clinical footer on every page.
