Level Badges & Digital Seals

Square medallions for email signatures, LinkedIn and the website, built as scalable SVG so they stay crisp at any size and export cleanly to transparent PNG at 512 and 1024 px. Each bears the Arabic glyph, level numeral and the English designation in a tight ring of DM Sans small caps. Gold deepens with seniority; the Ijāzah badge is struck fully in gold.

Export. Each badge is vector. To produce the deliverable transparent PNGs at 512 px and 1024 px (and an .svg), render the medallion alone on a transparent canvas. The coin face is opaque so it reads on any background; the legibility strip above confirms the glyph and numeral hold at 64 px.
