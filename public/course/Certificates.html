Level Certificates

One master template, rendered as five mentor-track faces from Asās to Ijāzah. A4 landscape (297 × 210 mm). All copy lives in the editable LEVELS data; the level accent deepens with seniority and gold is reserved for the Ijāzah seal. Each face carries the recipient field, bilingual level name, award title, issue date, certificate number, signature line and level seal.
